What is a Digital Twin?

Connecting the physical and the digital is not just a cconcept anymore;Digital twin refers to a dynamic simulation of a real-life object or system created by real-time data collected from the object being modeled. This means that it is not a stationary model but an evolving one where the behavior and condition of the actual object or system is mirrored. Digital twin facilitates industries to know their future and be able to plan based on what would happen in the future. Thus, it bridges the gap between the real and virtual world.
